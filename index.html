Why Shakti Rice?

Located in Kichha, Uttarakhand, Shri Shakti Rice & Dal Mills has been a cornerstone of quality rice manufacturing and distribution. We pride ourselves on delivering premium-grade rice products that meet the highest standards of quality and purity.
